Welcome to Birdie Dum Dums Suport Site!

Please read below for the most common FAQ:

How do I play?

The point of the game is to get as far as you can before the speed becomes too much for you to navigate. You do this by using your device's internal accelerometer by tilting your phone left and right to move Birdie out of the way of oncoming obstacles.

Why can't I use touch control for gameplay?

Birdie Dum Dums was built for use with accelerometers, and currently there are no controls for using touch screen to move Birdie. Possibly in the future, so check back often.

How do I beat the game?

Birdie Dum Dums is unbeatable. The point of the game is to get as far as you can before the speed becomes too much for you to navigate.

Why is the game so hard?

Practice makes perfect. And by perfect I mean good luck.
